Mission Mango Squirrel

  • Support for "Mission Mango" initiated by environmentalist Subhajit Mukherjee
  • Inspired by the squirrel's legendary contribution to Lord Ram's mission.
  • Encourages participation from individuals, organizations, and schools on making a "Squirrel Contribution".
  • Invites people across India to join and contribute towards this mission.

Credit: Pngkit  

About Mission Mango

  • Initiated by environmentalist Subhajit Mukherjee since 2020
  • Increasing trees in the environment
  • Boosting mango fruit production in India. Encourage Fruit Based Cultivation.
  • Encourage Green Cover of farm land by reducing Paddy Based Cultivation.
  • Promote Ground Water Recharge.
Credit: Subhajit Mukherjee  

About Subhajit Mukherjee

Known as "The Green Man," Subhajit Mukherjee is an environmental activist and the founder of Mission Green Mumbai. His efforts include:

  • Planting over 63,000 trees in Mumbai
  • Promoting rainwater harvesting techniques
  • Educating thousands on environmental conservation
  • Winning awards like the Jal Rakshak Award from the Government of India
Photo by Zdeněk Macháček on Unsplash  

About Squirrel Contribution

A "Squirrel Contribution" means joining the Mission Mango movement by:

  • Spreading awareness about the mission
  • Collecting mango seeds and contributing them. How to Contribute?
  • Replicating the model locally to support farmers
Credit: Get Bengal Website  

How to Contribute?

5 step process

  1. Eat a Mango! The best part!
  2. Collect the Seed and Wash it Thoroughly.
  3. Dry it in the Sun.
  4. Store it in a Dry Box [Cardboard is best, not plastic!]
  5. Send the box [Message for more details on mission.mango.squirrel ]
